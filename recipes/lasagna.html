Lasagne

Description

An italian classic layered mince or alternative with veg, pasta sheets and white sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Passata
  • White sauce
  • Mince or veg alternative
  • Choice of veg

Steps

  1. Brown mince or alternative with veg adding passata to finish
  2. Layer items cooked items, pasta sheets then white sauce repeating
  3. Bake for 45 minutes
